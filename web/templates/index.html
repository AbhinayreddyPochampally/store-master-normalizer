What this tool does

You upload one or more brand monthly exports plus the backend master. The tool reads each brand's mapping rules from brands.json and reconciles every store cell-by-cell, ignoring cosmetic differences like case, type, or date format.

You get back, per brand, an updated master and a change report. Aggregate counts across all selected brands appear in the validation summary.

Nothing is sent anywhere — it all happens locally on your computer.
