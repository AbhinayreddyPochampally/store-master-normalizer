How it works

v3 reads each workbook's sheet names on upload and auto-selects via four rules — last-used → preferred → largest → first. You can override any pick. Brand exports are folded into the master in the order shown. Output: one cumulative Updated_Master_<Month>.xlsx plus a per-brand Change_Report. Nothing leaves the browser.
